FAQs Frequently Asked Questions

How to create a new bookmark in this app ?

Its easy to do with these following steps :

1. Create a category if you haven't created yet,
2. Open the category and click on add new bookmark,
3. Fill the form with title, link and description,
4. Click on save.

Why shall I use this app instead of browser bookmark ?

We provide customizable, maintainable and categorized data entry feature that a browser usually doesn't provide.
